Upload a thermal image

As part of the CHEESE project, we want to record thermal images that:

  • clearly display a building construction fault;
  • clearly display a the effect of insulation or draughtproofing;
  • or show anything else of interest or out of the ordinary.
From our combined survey experience, we hope to build a collection of common faults and remedies, that can be used as a reference by housholders after a survey, and indeed to anyone else. The thermal images page displays the images we have collected so far.

Please use this page to upload images, providing with each one a short description of what the image illustrates and your name. Do not upload any images that identify a specific person or place.
