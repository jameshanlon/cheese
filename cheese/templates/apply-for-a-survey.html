Apply for a home survey

Please complete the form below to apply for a survey, but before you do, we ask that you read through the survey preparation and information guide and are happy to make the necessary preparations for the survey, and to report your progress back to us after one month and after one year.

Note that The CHEESE Project offers surveys to anyone in Bristol, but we do concentrate on six wards in Bristol: Bishopston, Cotham, Easton, Filwood (Knowle West), Lawrence Weston and Redland. Since we are a small volunteer-led organisation, the number of surveys we can do is limited and they will be offered on a first-come first-served basis and depend on the scheduling with other surveys and travel time and costs.

Once you have submitted the form, we will be in contact to organise a date with you. If you have any questions about our surveys, then please get in touch by emailing surveys@cheeseproject.co.uk.
