Notice on survey availability

Our home thermal-imaging surveys are currently suspended due to the pandemic and the necessity to work inside with the windows closed (see more details on our survey procedure). We are monitoring the situation and Government guidance and are poised to recommence surveying as soon as it is safe to do so.

Our surveys normally run from October through to April to obtain an 8 degree temperature difference inside to outside. If you would like to make a booking for the 2021/22 winter season, then please use our booking form and we will be in touch as soon as we can offer you a survey.

Please complete the form below to apply for a survey, but before you do, we ask that you read through the survey preparation and information guide and are happy to make the necessary preparations for the survey, and to report your progress back to us after one month and after one year.

CHEESE Project surveys are available to anyone in the greater Bristol area and we work with community-based groups to deliver surveys in Bishopston, Cotham, Easton, Filwood (Knowle West), Lawrence Weston, Lockleaze, Redland, and Westbury-on-Trym. Since we are a small volunteer-led organisation, the number of surveys we can do is limited and they will be offered on a first-come first-served basis and depend on the scheduling with other surveys and travel time and costs.

Details of the survey process and pricing can be found on this page.

Once you have submitted the form, we will be in contact to organise a date with you. If you have any questions about our surveys, then please get in touch by emailing surveys@cheeseproject.co.uk.
