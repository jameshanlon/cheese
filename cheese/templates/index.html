Save money and improve the comfort of your home with a CHEESE survey

The Cold Homes Energy Efficiency Survey Experts (CHEESE) Project is a Bristol-based not-for-profit community enterprise that aims to reduce domestic energy losses, at low cost. Our thermal-imaging surveys identify where your home is losing heat and we will suggest remedies. This saves you money, improves your comfort and wellbeing, and reduces your contribution to climate change. Read more about how a survey works, and if you live in Bristol, apply for a survey now.
