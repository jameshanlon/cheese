Award winning thermal imaging surveys for Bristol homes
Get all the detail, save money, save carbon, keep warm

The Cold Homes Energy Efficiency Survey Experts (C.H.E.E.S.E.) Project is a Bristol-based not-for-profit CIC that aims to reduce domestic energy losses, at low cost: surveys start at just £100 and are free to those on low incomes. Our unique thermal-imaging surveys identify where your home is losing heat and we will suggest remedies. This saves you money, improves your comfort and wellbeing, and reduces your contribution to climate change. Read more about how a survey works or apply for a survey now.
