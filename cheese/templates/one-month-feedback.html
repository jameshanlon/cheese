One-month feedback

As part of the CHEESE survey process, we check in with householders one month and one year after the survey. After one month, we would like to know what you are thinking of doing to do reduce your energy consumption, as well as your previous energy usage and any feedback your have. This data is important for us to demonstrate the effectivness of what we do.
