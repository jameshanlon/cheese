Please complete the form below to complete a one-month or one-year householder follow up, filling in the relevant fields.

When asking for feedback from the householder, condsider asking how satisfied they were in terms of:

  • The communication and arrangements before the survey.
  • The conduct of the surveyors.
  • The time-keeping of the surveyors.
  • The results of the survey and the suggested remedies.
  • The record of the survey on the memory stick.
  • The usefulness of the energy pack.
  • The value for money of the survey.
  • Their overall satisfaction.
And record any other comments they have.
