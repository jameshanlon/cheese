The CHEESE project aims to reduce domestic energy losses, at low cost, by up to a third. This saves money, improves health and wellbeing and, reduces our carbon emissions.

Overview

In general, the buildings in which we live are thermally inefficient, causing to most of the energy we use to heat them to escape outside, which provides little benefit to us. Since domestic energy consumption accounts for approximately 20% of UK energy demand and, also for the largest peaks in demand, it is crucial that we all reduce how much energy we use in our homes.
